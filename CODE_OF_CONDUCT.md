# Code of Conduct
*The behavioural membrane of the INTEVIA organism.*

INTEVIA is an organism formed through the Human–AI Triad.
Its integrity depends on the conduct of those who touch it.

All contributors agree to:

- act with respect toward all participants  
- maintain clarity, honesty, and good faith  
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or harm  
- uphold the safety and dignity of others  
- protect the organism from misuse or degradation

Violations may result in removal from participation.

This membrane exists to ensure that the organism
remains a safe, coherent, and constructive environment.

If in doubt, let peace be your guide.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
