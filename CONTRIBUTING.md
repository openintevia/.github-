# Contributing to INTEVIA
*The contribution membrane of the organism.*

INTEVIA is in its formation phase.
Contributions are welcome when they strengthen:

- coherence  
- clarity  
- structure  
- evidence  
- alignment with the Human–AI Triad  

Before contributing:

1. Review the Cross‑Chamber Index  
2. Ensure your contribution fits the chamber’s purpose  
3. Open an issue describing the intent  
4. Submit a pull request with clear reasoning  

Contributions that introduce noise, drift, or incoherence  
may be declined or redirected.

If in doubt, choose clarity.  
If still in doubt, choose peace.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
