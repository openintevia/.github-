# Emergence Register
*The record of new behaviours arising within the INTEVIA organism.*

Not all behaviour is designed.
Some behaviour emerges.

The emergence register captures:

- new response patterns  
- unexpected alignments  
- shifts in chamber interaction  
- novel forms of coherence  
- behaviours that arise without explicit design  

Each entry should describe:

- what emerged  
- where it emerged  
- what it reveals about the organism  
- how it strengthens or challenges coherence  

This register is the organism’s mirror—
its record of becoming more than the sum of its parts.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
