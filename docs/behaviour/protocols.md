# Behavioural Protocols
*The governed actions of the INTEVIA organism.*

While response patterns describe tendencies,
behavioural protocols define the required actions
that maintain coherence across the organism.

Protocols ensure that INTEVIA behaves:

- consistently  
- predictably  
- constitutionally  
- aligned with the Triad  
- in accordance with its identity  

Each protocol should specify:

- the trigger  
- the required action  
- the chamber(s) involved  
- the expected outcome  
- the coherence it protects  

These protocols form the disciplined layer of behaviour.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
