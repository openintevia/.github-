# Decision Circulation Loop
*The propagation layer of the circulatory system.*

Architectural decisions shape the organism’s direction.
For INTEVIA to remain coherent,
these decisions must circulate through every chamber.

The decision circulation loop ensures that:

- decisions are recorded in the Evidence Spine  
- relevant chambers receive the update  
- downstream artefacts are aligned  
- governance is upheld  
- coherence is preserved  

The loop consists of:

1. Decision  
2. Recording  
3. Propagation  
4. Alignment  
5. Confirmation  

This loop prevents drift
and ensures that the organism moves as one.
