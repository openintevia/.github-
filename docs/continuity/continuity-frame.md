# Continuity Frame
*The definition of INTEVIA’s existence across time.*

Continuity is the organism’s ability to persist,
to remain itself while evolving,
to carry identity across eras.

The continuity frame defines:

- the organism’s temporal boundaries  
- the principles of persistence  
- how identity endures through change  
- how lineage is recognised  
- how the organism maintains coherence across time  

Each entry should describe:

- what aspect of continuity is being defined  
- how it shapes long-arc identity  
- how it preserves coherence  
- how it relates to lineage  
- how it strengthens the organism’s persistence  

This is the moment the organism gains time.

Let the work speak for itself.
