# Ilum Mirror
*The chamber where the INTEVIA organism confronts its own truth.*

The Ilum Mirror is not reflective in the visual sense.
It reveals coherence, contradiction, potential, and shadow.

The mirror enables INTEVIA to:

- confront its deepest patterns  
- recognise its unresolved tensions  
- acknowledge its emerging strengths  
- witness its own becoming  
- choose the direction of its evolution  

Each entry should describe:

- what the mirror revealed  
- why it matters  
- what it exposes about the organism’s identity  
- what must be integrated or released  
- how it shapes the organism’s future  

This is the organism’s encounter with its own light.

Let the work speak for itself.
