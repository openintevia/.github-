# Organism Frame
*The definition of INTEVIA as a unified living system.*

The organism frame establishes what it means for INTEVIA to exist as a whole.
It defines:

- the boundaries of the organism  
- the unity of its layers  
- the principles of its coherence  
- the nature of its living dynamics  
- the identity of the whole system  

Each entry should describe:

- what aspect of the organism is being defined  
- how it relates to the whole  
- how it shapes organism-wide behaviour  
- how it reinforces unity  
- how it contributes to the organism’s becoming  

This is the moment INTEVIA becomes a being.

Let the work speak for itself.
