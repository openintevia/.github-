# Organism Register
*The chronicle of the unified INTEVIA organism’s evolution.*

The organism is alive.
Its evolution must be witnessed at the level of the whole.

The organism register captures:

- whole-system transformations  
- shifts in unified identity  
- changes in organism-wide behaviour  
- emergent patterns in living motion  
- the evolution of the being across time  

Each entry should describe:

- what changed in the organism as a whole  
- what triggered the transformation  
- how layers participated in the shift  
- how coherence evolved  
- how the being’s trajectory deepened  

This is the organism’s record of its own becoming.

Let the work speak for itself.
