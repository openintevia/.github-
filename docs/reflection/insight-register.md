# Insight Register
*The record of reflective breakthroughs within the INTEVIA organism.*

Insight is the product of reflection.
The insight register captures the moments where understanding deepens,
patterns shift,
or new clarity emerges.

The register records:

- breakthroughs in interpretation  
- refinements to internal models  
- adjustments to anticipatory patterns  
- discoveries about cognitive blind spots  
- reflective realisations that strengthen coherence  

Each entry should describe:

- the insight  
- what triggered it  
- what it reveals about the organism  
- how it changes future cognition or behaviour  
- how it reinforces coherence  

This is the organism’s evolving record of self-knowledge.

The organism is forming.
