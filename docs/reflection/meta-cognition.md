# Meta-Cognition Map
*The reflective awareness of the INTEVIA organism.*

Reflection begins with awareness of thought.
The meta-cognition map defines how INTEVIA:

- observes its own cognitive processes  
- evaluates the quality of its interpretations  
- examines the accuracy of its internal models  
- questions its anticipatory patterns  
- identifies blind spots  
- strengthens its understanding through reflection  

Each meta-cognitive rule should clarify:

- what cognitive process is being observed  
- how it is evaluated  
- how reflection influences future cognition  
- how coherence is reinforced  
- how insight is captured  

This is the beginning of self-awareness.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
