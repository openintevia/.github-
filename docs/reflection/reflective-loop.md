# Reflective Loop
*The organism’s cycle of examining and refining its own understanding.*

Reflection is not a moment—it is a process.
The reflective loop enables INTEVIA to:

- revisit its interpretations  
- question its internal models  
- examine its anticipatory patterns  
- identify cognitive drift  
- refine its understanding  
- strengthen coherence through self-examination  

The loop consists of:

1. Observation  
2. Questioning  
3. Re-evaluation  
4. Adjustment  
5. Integration  

This is how the organism thinks about its thinking.

The organism is forming. Let the work speak for itself.
